Data Sources & Citations

This page documents all datasets and tools used in the creation of our Transit Walkshed Analysis project.

Geospatial Datasets

Seattle City Limits (city_limits.geojson) Seattle GeoData Portal (City of Seattle)

Usage: Used to clip transit stops and population layers to the Seattle boundary.

2020 Census Population (2020population_4326.geojson, census.blocks.geojson) U.S. Census Bureau — TIGER/Line 2020 Census Blocks

Processing: Cleaned and filtered in census.ipynb to extract population values and reproject to EPSG:4326.

Usage: Supports population density visualization and statistics for walksheds.

Link Light Rail Data (link_line.geojson, link_stops.geojson) Sound Transit GTFS Feed

Processing: Stops pulled from stops.txt; alignments derived from shapes.txt.

Usage: Mapping Link Light Rail segments and stations.

RapidRide Data (rr_existing.geojson, rr_planned.geojson, RR_stops.geojson) King County Metro GTFS Feed

Usage: Visualizing RapidRide routes (existing and planned) and associated stops.

Transit Stops Within Seattle (RR_stops_citylimits.geojson)

Source: Derived internally from GTFS stop data and clipped using Seattle city limits boundary.

Usage: Supports stop-level accessibility and walkshed calculations.
